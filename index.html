Simulations

Hodgkin-Huxley Neuron

Real-time simulation of a single neuron using the 1952 Hodgkin-Huxley model (Na⁺/K⁺ channels). Each click injects 15 μA/cm² for 1 ms — enough to trigger an action potential.

Stigmergic Swarm

Eight agents (researcher, coder, reasoner, memory) coordinate without a central controller. Agents deposit pheromone task markers, seek nearby signals, and leave result traces — emergent collective behavior from simple local rules.
